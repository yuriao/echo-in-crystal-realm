Privacy Policy for Echo in the Crystal Realm

Information We Collect:

  • Account Information: Email address when you sign up or log in.
  • Chat Content: Messages in the App, stored to provide chat functionality.
  • Crash & Diagnostics: Device information, crash logs, and app usage data.
  • Purchase Data: Subscription and purchase history (via Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat).

How We Use Information:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts.
  • Provide and maintain chat functionality.
  • Process subscriptions and purchases through Apple and RevenueCat.
  • Diagnose crashes, improve stability, and enhance performance.

Data Sharing:

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted providers that enable core app features:

  • Apple (App Store, In-App Purchase): Payment processing.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and purchase verification.
  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, analytics, and crash reporting.

Each provider processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Your Choices:

  • You can request account deletion by using the in-app “Delete Account” option. All account information will be deleted

Data Retention:

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the App or comply with legal obligations.
